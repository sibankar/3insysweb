The Importance of Security Management
The data that companies create, exchange, and store is one of their most valuable assets.

Whether data is being accessed by a customer through a portal or directly by an employee on the internal network, information security and privacy is needed to protect the data while enabling individuals to easily access and have control over their data.

In today’s hybrid environment with users having access to their data through multiple devices and applications, increasing security without making it overly complex is critical to delivering a good customer experience.

The Cross System Integrator® [XSI]
XSI Delivers Next Generation System Integration

To address the needs of modern integration efforts, the Cross System Integrator [XSI] is a complete system integration solution that makes Information Technology (IT) and Operations Technology (OT) integration as simple as plug-and-play.

XSI delivers an end-to-end approach to system integration that reduces risk, shortens implementation time, increases operational efficiency, and reduces integration costs.
