Cookie Policy

Effective Date: November 2020

Cookies are small files of numbers and letters that we transfer to your computer’s hard drive. Some cookies used on this site are essential and are needed to ensure that the site works correctly, such as maintaining the background image that you see on each web page.

We also use some non-essential cookies to collect data about the number of visitors and user interaction with the site and to understand and save preferences for future visits.
